# Quid Fintech Design System

## Aesthetic Direction

Modern fintech SaaS aesthetic inspired by Stripe, Ramp, Linear, Revolut, and Notion. Premium, trustworthy, sophisticated with AI-guardian positioning rather than traditional budgeting app.

## Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- Deep Navy (#0A0E27) - primary background, headers
- Electric Blue (#3B82F6, #2563EB) - primary actions, accents
- Purple (#8B5CF6, #7C3AED) - secondary accents, AI highlights
- Clean White (#FFFFFF) - content backgrounds

**Supporting Colors:**
- Navy variants for depth and layering
- Subtle gradients combining blue and purple
- Glassmorphism effects with semi-transparent backgrounds

## Typography

**Display Font:** Inter (700-800 weight for hero headings)
**Body Font:** Inter (400-500 weight for readable content)
**Mono Font:** JetBrains Mono for financial data, numbers, status codes

**Hierarchy:**
- Hero headlines: 56-72px, bold
- Section headlines: 36-48px, semibold
- Subsection headlines: 24-32px, medium
- Body text: 16-18px, regular
- Captions: 14px, regular

## Visual Treatment

**Glassmorphism:**
- Semi-transparent backgrounds with backdrop-blur
- Subtle borders with rgba opacity
- Layered depth with multiple glass panels

**Gradients:**
- Subtle mesh gradients in backgrounds
- Blue-to-purple gradients for accents
- Radial gradients for spotlight effects

**Shadows:**
- Soft, layered shadows for depth
- Colored glows for interactive elements
- Avoid harsh drop shadows

## Layout Principles

- Generous whitespace (80-120px section padding)
- Asymmetric compositions when appropriate
- Grid-based structure with intentional breaking
- Full-bleed hero sections
- Content max-width: 1200-1400px

## Trust Indicators

- Security badges prominently displayed
- Bank-level encryption messaging
- Financial institution logos
- Testimonials with real photos
- Transparent pricing
- Clear data privacy statements

## Animations & Micro-interactions

- Smooth scroll-triggered animations
- Fade-in on viewport entry
- Hover state transitions (200-300ms)
- Number count-ups for financial data
- Card lift effects on hover
- Subtle parallax for depth

## Responsive Behavior

- Mobile-first approach
- Breakpoint at 1024px for tablet
- Breakpoint at 768px for mobile
- Stack cards vertically on small screens
- Collapsible navigation

## Brand Voice

- Confident but not arrogant
- Intelligent but not complex
- Protective but not controlling
- "Your AI financial guardian" positioning
